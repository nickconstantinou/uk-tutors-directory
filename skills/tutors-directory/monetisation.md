## Implementation

### Step 1: Lead Form
Simple form on site:
- Parent name
- Child's age
- Subject needed
- Location
- Budget
- Contact preference

### Step 2: Lead Distribution
- Match tutors by subject + location
- Send to 3 tutors (tenders model)
- First to pay gets the lead

### Step 3: Payment
- Stripe for card payments
- Invoice for larger leads
- PayPal for tutors who prefer it

### Step 4: Tracking
- Dashboard for tutors: views, leads, conversion
- Analytics: which subjects/locations convert best
